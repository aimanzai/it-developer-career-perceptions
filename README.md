# it-developer-career-perceptions

## Overview
Nowadays, software developers play a crucial role in shaping the technological foundation of businesses and society. As the IT industry has evolved rapidly due to fast-changing in technology on a global scale has significantly transformed how developers work and perceive their careers. Factors such as remote work trends, tech industry experiences, educational backgrounds, and weekly working hours have emerged as main elements that can affect developersâ€™ jobs and overall career satisfaction. By understanding these factors, it can help to identify valuable insights for employers, managers, and the IT industry in general to enhance their developers' well-being and productivitye. Hence, this analysis provides richer insights into how different backgrounds shape career perception in the IT field.

## Objectives
The primary goal of this analysis is to gain valuable insights into how developers' perceptions of overall career and job satisfaction vary based on different factors. Moreover, to understand how these variations can impact their contribution to the IT industry.

## Data Science Questions
1. Are there any significant influences on job satisfaction for developers who work remotely across the Top 3 countries?
2. Do developers from different educational backgrounds view their careers differently?
3. Is there any significance between years of professional coding experience and career satisfaction?
4. How does the work location of developers affect their opinions on the profession?
5. How do working hours per week influence developers' enjoyment status on their jobs?
6. How does work challenge affect developers' perception of career fulfillment?
